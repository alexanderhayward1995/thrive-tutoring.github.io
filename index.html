About Ieuan

An experienced teacher who gets it

I'm a qualified primary school teacher (PGCE, Outstanding) with years of classroom experience across Reception to Year 6. I've worked as a class teacher and Maths Lead, and now as a specialist 1-to-1 and small group tutor in North Bristol.

My particular specialism is Year 6 SATs preparation — helping children walk into the reading, maths, and SPaG papers feeling calm, well-practised, and confident. As a former Maths Lead I know exactly what the papers are asking for and how to close gaps quickly without the pressure.

Alongside core subjects and SATs prep, I have extensive experience supporting neurodivergent children — whether your child needs a confidence boost or specialist ADHD and autism support, my approach is calm, flexible, and built around their strengths.

For your peace of mind, I am fully insured with Public Liability Insurance, registered with the ICO for data protection, and hold an enhanced DBS check. As a dad of three, I understand the juggle. I also know that every child learns differently — and that the right support, at the right pace, can make all the difference.
